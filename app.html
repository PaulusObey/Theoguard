TheoGuard

A lightweight app for Christian teaching evaluation and biblical discernment

🚀 Launch the App

🌟 Mission

TheoGuard exists to help everyday Christians test teachings, understand sound doctrine, and grow in discernment using simple, accessible tools.

🚀 Features

🔍 Teaching Checker

Copy and paste sermons or phrases. Get instant flags for false teachings with Scripture references.

📖 Doctrine Library

Explore foundational Christian doctrines and learn how they relate to heresies in history.

🎤 Audio Transcript

Upload sermon recordings and preview text transcripts (simulated for now, AI integration planned).

📱 Installable App

Works offline. Add to your home screen and take discernment with you anywhere.
